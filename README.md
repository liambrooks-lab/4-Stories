# 4 Stories

A dark, twisted, and emotional story collection made with pure HTML, CSS & JavaScript.

Four completely different stories — each hitting different feelings: love, fear, suspicion, and pure madness.

## 📖 The Stories

### ❤️ **The Last Cigarette** (Romantic & Emotional)
A deeply moving love story about memories, loss, and the final cigarette that burns with their last promises.

### 🕵️ **The Wrong Number** (Suspicious / Psychological Thriller)
Someone is watching you. They know what you’re doing right now... and even what you’ll do tomorrow.

### 😂 **The Ghost Who Failed JEE** (Dark Comedy)
The most annoying ghost in India — a frustrated JEE aspirant who failed horribly and now haunts students by teaching at 3 AM.

### 🩸 **He Carved Off His Own Reflection** (Extreme NSFL Horror)
Extremely graphic body horror. Flesh, mirrors, blood, and insanity. Not for the faint-hearted.

## ✨ Features

- Cinematic dark paper aesthetic with bloody vibes
- Unique "Enter the Story" experience for each tale
- Fully responsive design
- Ambient sound for the horror story
- Pure vanilla HTML, CSS & JavaScript (no frameworks)

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Clone the repository or download the zip
2. Open `index.html` in your browser
3. Choose your poison

**Warning:** The horror story contains extreme gore and disturbing content. Reader discretion is strongly advised.
